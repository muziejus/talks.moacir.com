## Theory DH?

> The question is not: is it true? But: does it work? What new thoughts does it make it possible to think? What new emotions does it make it possible to feel? What new sensations and perceptions does it open in the body?

B. Massumi, “Translator’s Foreword,” _A Thousand Plateaus_

## Global DH?

> A history of digital text undoubtedly reflects the legacy of European and, later, North American colonialism. Treaties that negotiated early character encodings initially did so in the dominant diplomatic languages, French and English.

D. Tenen, _Plain Text_
